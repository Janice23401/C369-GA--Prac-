Frequently Asked Questions

How do I top up my digital wallet?
Click on the 'Top Up' button in your digital wallet page and follow the instructions to add funds using your credit card.
How do I make a payment?
Navigate to the payment section, enter the amount and other required details, and click on 'Make Payment'.
What should I do if my payment fails?
If your payment fails, please check your card details and ensure you have sufficient funds. If the issue persists, contact our support team through the 'Need Help' form.
